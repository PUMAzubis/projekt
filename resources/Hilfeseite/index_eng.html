About us



What is the BVS?

The BVS is a purley DPMA internal application, that is made exclusively for the use of employees of the German Patent und Trade Mark Office.
The BVS lets users search for books und borrow them. Librarians have a wider range of functions available.



Why the BVS?

This application was created by the trainees of the first year of education, to abolish the partially chaotic index carding system in the library and also to relieve the staff employed there.



What can the BVS do?

After registrating successfully, employees can search for books and borrow them.
Librarians can search for books in the stock and borrow them out to other employees.
They can also register new books, edit existing books or delete them. Librarians can also create new employees, edit employees or delete them.



Login & Registration


How can I registrate myself?

To be able to use the BVS you need to have an User Account. To registrate yourself, press the "Registrate"-Button in the "Login"-Window.

Now you are in the "Registration"-Window. The requiered fields Identification Number, First name, Last name and Password need to be filled out truthfully.

Identification number: Type in your DPMA internal identification number, you can find it on the front side of your time card.

Password: Type in a save Password with at least 8 characters.

After filling out all fields properly, please press the "Complete Registration"-Button.

Please wait until your registration was confirmed & validated by one of the administrators.
This usually takes 1-2 working days.



How can I log myself in?

If you already have a User Account you can log yourself in.

To do so, please type in your DPMA internal identification number and the password you chosed, after that click on the"Login"-Button.



Forgot your password?

If you forgot the password to your personal User Account, press the "Forgot your Password?"-Button in the "Login"-Window.

Now you should be in the "Forgot your Password"-Window. Here you can find a list of Email adresses of the currently available administrators. Contact one of the administrators in the list via Email and describe your problem. So that there won't be any problems in the processing of your problem, please make sure to provide your first and last name as well as your identification number. The responsible administrator will reset your password as quick as possible and send a temporary password to you.



How can I log myself out?

After your session in the BVS you should log yourself out. To do so, click on the upper menu bar, then on the menu item "User" and then on the menu item "Logout" to log out of the system.



How can I change my password?

For various security reasons, you should change your password regularly.

To change your password, press the "User" tab in the top menu bar.

Select the menu item "Change password" to access the password change function. You can see three required fields, "Old password", "New password" and "Confirm new password".

Old password: Enter your old password here, if you forget this, use the "Forgot your password" function.

New Password: Enter your new password, here please note that it does not correspond to the old password and must contain at least 8 characters. Passwords such as "password" or "12345678" should not be used for security reasons.

Confirm new password: Confirm your new password by typing it repeatedly.

Please keep in mind that your password is case-sensetive.

Functions


How can I search for books?

After successfully logging in you will see the menu. As a DPMA employee, the "Search" and "Borrow" functions are available.

To search for an existing book, press the search box next to the text "Search:".

Now enter either the ISBN-10, the title, the author, or the year of release of the book.

The search results can now be viewed on the right, the book you are looking for is included in the list, highlight it with a left-click and then press the "Borrow" button to book the book with a deadline of up to Three months.



Menu-Functions as librarian?

Librarians of the DPMA can also use the functions "loan", "new book", "edit", and "delete" except for the search and borrowing of books.

Lend: After searching for a book in the search bar, select it by left-clicking with the mouse and then press "Lend". Now you can select a user to which you want to assign the selected book.

New book: If you want to enter a new book that is not available in the database, press "New book". Enter the title, author, ISBN, year of release and category and press "Save".

Edit: If you want to edit an existing book, press "Edit" after you have left-clicked the book you want to edit. Change the title, author, ISBN, year of release and/or category at will. To apply the changes, press "Save" as usual.

Delete: The Delete function lets you delete an existing book from the database irrevocably. To do this, select the book you want to delete by left-clicking and then press the "Delete" button. Confirm the "Are you sure?" Dialog box with "Yes" to permanently remove the book from the database.



Book not existing?

First, make sure your search criteria are correct.
Is the author's name correct?
Is the year of release correct?
Did you enter the right ISBN?

The DPMA's BVS uses the ISBN-10 for your search functions, and the inclusion of ISBN-13 is still being planned.
The ISBN-10 is characterized by the fact that it consists exclusively of 10 digits, excluding the hyphens.
